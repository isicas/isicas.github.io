ISICAS 2021 Welcome Message from the General Chair

Dear friends,

This year has been very difficult for conference organizers because of the Covid-19 pandemic. The pandemic has forced us to completely rethink the way we organize our conferences with uncertainties about the format - i.e. virtual, hybrid or in person, the expected number of participants, the impact on the contracts and the budget that need to be continuously adapted, just to name a few highlights.

We took the risk of moving from the in person format to the hybrid format because in the meantime, vaccines have been introduced and some countries, including Singapore, have gradually begun to relax the entry restrictions in their countries. There are still the restrictions of the return and the fear of the virus that I hope will gradually dissipate.

We would like to thank the TPC Co-Chairs (Elena, Edoardo, Gabriele and Tim) and the reviewers behind the scene, an extraordinary job has been done to select the papers that will be published in their journals, the Organizing Committee (Jun Wei, Sai Hua and Kwen Siong) who have been working very hard on ensuring the smoothness in the logistics and budget and the Publicity Co-Chairs Yongfu and Hadi for their hard work in developing a nice website and for promoting the conference in the social networks.

We have chosen Marina Bay Sand (http://www.ieee-isicas.org/hotel/index.html), one of the best hotels in Singapore to host our conference and I hope many of you will make the trip to attend in person. Please book before November 19th to enjoy discounted rates.

Vaccinated travelers can enjoy quarantine-free travel between Singapore and seven European countries (Denmark, France, Germany, Italy, the Netherlands, Spain, and the United Kingdom), Canada, and the United States, with South Korea starting 15 November 2021. More details in the link: https://safetravel.ica.gov.sg/vtl/requirements-and-process

We have prepared a high level technical program with three keynotes on December 9th open to everyone free of charge, a one day live sessions and one day virtual sessions. The program still needs to be adjusted depending on the number of authors attending in person. More details in the link: http://www.ieee-isicas.org/program/index.html

We look forward to welcoming you to this beautiful country with its exemplary hospitality tradition and rich culinary variety. A close friend who moved to Singapore a few years ago told me Singapore is a paradise for children. So don't hesitate to bring your children, there are so many good surprises for them.

We look forward to meeting you in person or virtually in Singapore.

General Co-Chairs Amara, Bah Hwee and Franco

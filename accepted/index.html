ISICAS 2020 Aurthor Instructions

Congratulations on your acceptance into ISICAS 2020. The authors will be given the opportunity to submit a presentation video through the ISICAS Virtual Platform http://isicas2020-virtual.org. Instructions on how to access the virtual platform will be emailed to paid attendees the week prior to the start of the conference.

Accepted Authors must complete the following tasks:

  • Submit a video presentation, Please check out the Presentation Instruction and complete the video preparation by 24th July 2020.
  • Pay registration fees, Please check out the Registration and complete the registration by 31th July 2020.(To be available soon)
  • Prepare the live session, Please check out the Live Sessions(To be finalized soon)
  • Respond to all Q&A within 24 hours of the question being posted
